The Overweight Snail

The once was a snail who, when put on a scale,
Weighed three thousand pounds and a quarter.
"Dear me!" said she, "I'll lose weight in the sea.
"I simply must stay in the water."

So with this silly notion she jumped in the ocean
With the thought of turning out trim.
But she found out too late as she dived to her fate
That she was unable to swim.

-- Matt 1964
