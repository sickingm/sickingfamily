To Elaine, on Her Natal Anniversary

Oh, our Lainey's turning fifty
And we think that's pretty nifty
And her brood wants her to know that she is great.
So Andy got a limo
And then Laura sent a memo
And the DBC showed up to celebrate.

Though we found no dancing midgets
(Lil' Rascal's Stymie called them "fidgets")
Nor did Yellow Pages show a Rent-a-Dwarf
Maggie brought food as her task
And Louie found some monkey masks
And I promise not to eat so much I barf.

Twice Elaine got me a prom date
Without her I never would rate
Come to think of it, I even wore her dress.
Though Jim never asked me out
Only two years did I pout
But we're here to toast Elaine, and I digress.

Our universes are colliding
Laine's free time went into hiding
And her new job seems to be just one big pain.
Still we'll raise our glass in toast
To our sister who's the most
As we shout our glad refrain, "We Love Elaine!"

With love from Clare!

-- Clare 12/19/2007
