To Paul On Account Of It's His Birthday

Famous people like Lauren Bacall,
Humphrey Bogart, and Monty Hall,
Brian Flynn and Denny Wall,
Mortimer Snerd and Arlene Dahl,
Have gathered in places like Senegal,
East Tel Aviv, and the Wailing Wall,
And a shopping mall in Montreal,
An upscale club in East Nepal,
And the Jehovah Witness Kingdom Hall.
(And six were crammed in a shower stall)
To make a congratulatory call
To my youngest brother, Sicking, Paul
But you were playing a game of hand ball.

Now it's up to me: "Happy Birthday, Paul."

-- Matt 6/7/06
