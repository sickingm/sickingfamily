To Laura

Laura 'Ginia, Sister Dear
On 12/29 of every year
It seems I'm always fated
To send birthday wishes on to you
However heartfelt, warm and true
Just a smidgeon late. It
Seems so understated
To say I appreciate it
That, although I know you hate it,
You've not recriminated
Me for being addlepated
Re this mess that I've created.
My transgression's not negated.
I know how long you've waited.
But as earlier I stated
I still send this card belated.

-- Matt 12/30/07
