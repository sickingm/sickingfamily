(This Poem Has No Title)

This is Jinny's birthday poem. It starts out kind of slow.
The later verses aren't much better as birthday verses go.

The rhyme scheme's nothing special, and the scansion's often wrong.
And it would take a magic melody to make this poem a song.

Though there is alliteration in the last foregoing line,
And close examination shows each couplet tends to rhyme.

But the whole poem lacks a message, not even "Happy birthday"
There's no real theme to speak of, and it lacks feeling in the worst way.

To say nothing of allusion or onomatopoeia.
In retrospect it seems to be just verbal diarrhea.

-- Matt Oct 2004
