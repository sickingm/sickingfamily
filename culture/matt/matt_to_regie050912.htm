To Regie On The Occasion of
Her Fiftieth Birthday

I got up this morning and ran in place,
Brushed my teeth and washed my face.
And as I stood and stared at the mirror
I saw on my cheek a glistening tear.
Not from my allergies, and not from fear,
It was caused by the weighty and utterly sheer
Impact of realizing with terribly clear
Insight as my mind finally got into gear
That my number two sister whom I think as a dear
Young lady with nary a peer
Was having a birthday again this year
Without us there to share in the cheer.
She's out there in Texas and meanwhile we're
Here in St. Louis drinking all of the beer

-- Matt 9/12/05
