Ode To Clare

Oh Clare, with your teeth shining silver and bright
Please help me find my way home late at night.

When all is dark and dreary, looking so murky and bleak,
I look for your mouth and hope to see its shining streak.

The brilliant glow that your metal sets forth
Guides me back like the star in the north.

Without your help where would I be
When I've boozed it up and hardly able to see.

Thank you for the service you perform
Like a guiding light in a terrible storm.

-- Elaine 1978
