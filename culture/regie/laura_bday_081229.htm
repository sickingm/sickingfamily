Many Happy Returns of Laura's Birthday

In splendor dawned this happy day,
(Yes, yesterday's tomorrow), ←for best results, pronounce this "tomorrah"
Ring in the joy, throw out the bad,
Dispose of all our sorrow, ←for best results, pronounce this "sorrah"
And celebrate with mirth the birth
Of our fair sister, Laura.

To those who still believe that she
Sold pots, I answer "Phooey!"
She's more the type, as most should know
To buy and sell the brewy.
I think that this exonerates
Our loony sister Louie.

Distraught that I can't share the day
In person, hear me blubber. ←for best results, pronounce this "blubbah"
To demonstrate my heartfelt love,
Oh would that I could rub a
Magic lantern, earn a wish,
For peerless sister Bubba.

-- Regie 12/29/08
