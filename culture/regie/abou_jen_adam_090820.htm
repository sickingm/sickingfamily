Abou Jen/Adam

Jeannette (and Adam - may their tribe increase!)
Awoke one night from peaceful sleep
And saw, bright as moonlight in her room,
Fifty-some candles dispelling the gloom,
And an angel writing in a book of gold.

Our dear Jeannette is always bold,
So to the presence in the room she said,
"What writest thou?"  The Vision raised her head,
And with a look of sweetest make
Said, "The names of those who love birthday cake."

"And is mine one?" asked Jeannette.  "Heck no,"
Replied the Angel.  Jeannette started to bellow,
But cheerily stopped; she said "Girl, you dream,
But write me down as one who loves ice cream."

The Angel wrote, and vanished. The next night
She came again with a great wakening light,
And showed the names whom love dessert best,
And, lo! Jeannette's name led all the rest!

-- Regie 8/20/09
