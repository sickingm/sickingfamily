For Nancy

Sing a song for Nancy,
Sixty-one
Loves to take a chance, she's
Lots of fun.

Long ago she made up
Songs and games
Never dreamed that now she'd
Be a gambling dame.

Take her down to Tunica,
Or the boats,
Indian casinos,
Harrah's moats.

Watch her pull the lever,
Smiles and tears,
Slot machine fever
Grips her recent years.

Temperature's not normal,
Things are hot
When you watch our Nancy
Feed a nickel slot.

Happy birthday, Nancy,
Sixty-one.
All your sibling fans, we
Hope you win some mon.
