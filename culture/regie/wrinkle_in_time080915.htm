A Wrinkle in Time

Dearest Clare,
Of memory fair--
OK, not fair but good.
Not good but great.
Can't overstate
Her mental gifts. Who could?

She knows it all
With great recall
Remembers family lore
From '63
To now, you see
(Perhaps even before).

Preventing rift
Here is my gift
On this, your natal day:
I'll now agree
We had A/C
On any date you say.

-- Regie 12/15/08
