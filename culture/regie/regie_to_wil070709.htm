Many Happy Returns

My brother Matt is known to you,
And also brother Paul.
And Mark, and all my sisters too,
Fine ladies, one and all.

A fact that you may learn with mirth,
A fact Matt may not own,
Is when a sibling's birth-
Day comes around, he writes a poem.

He blossoms forth, creative juice
Osmoses from his brains.
He fairly drips with poetry
He oozes out quatrains.

On Sicking Family dot com
He slings verse any time
One celebrates a natal day;
No sibling's safe from rhyme.

Your birthday's listed on our site
This 9th day of July
And yet no poem did Matthew write.
No posted ode. And why

Should you be left without some verse
Created by our Matt?
Matt's been remiss and I would think
He'd be ashamed of that.

Distinguished honorary bro' ,
Please have a happy day.
And Matt, it's time to do your du-
Ty. Write one right away.

-- Regie 07/09/07
