Birthday Reverie

Truly, dear Julie
I wish you the bestest
Alarm-clock-free morning,
French pancakes for breakfast,

Syrupy, buttery,
A la grand chef Mark
Chocolate all day,
From dawn until dark.

My wishes are dishes
Of ice cream and cream puffs,
The heck with that fiber
Drop that nutrition-stuff

Leave that for school days.
Hoist your cholesterol
Eat Danish pastry,
Slimed with a butter ball.

One Thousand Layer
Cake (might you please share?)
Top it all off with
A few peanut squares.

When sleepy, then deeply
Fall into a dream,
Of owning your very own
Store - Krispy Kreme.

-- Regie 9/13/08
