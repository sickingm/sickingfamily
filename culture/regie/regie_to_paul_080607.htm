06-07-08

On Six Seven Eight,
Most auspicious date,
May all of your wishes come true,
For hot dogs and Paydays,
Sun for the wedding day,
Skies that are shining and blue.

Cake all aflame
With wax dripped on your name
Inscribed in the icing upon it,
Fifty-five candles
That melt on your sandals -
Blow them out faster, doggone it.

On Six Seven Eight
May you be blessed by fate.
I send to you all my good wishes
I broadcast this call, Paul
And hope most of all, Paul,
That you will not have to do dishes

-- Regie 6/7/08
