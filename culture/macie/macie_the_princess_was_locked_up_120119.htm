The Princess was Locked Up

Once upon a time there was a princess locked in a castle.
There was a witch who locked her in there. The princess's name was Betty.
The witch did not like her name. When the princess was sleeping, the witch cursed Princess Betty.
The witch dared her to change her name to Evil Betty.
The princess changed her name to Evil Betty, and she woke up being evil.
The princess's fingers turned ugly and her nails were red and sharp.
The princess loved being evil and what she loved about being evil was her fingers and doing this:
[insert here a gesture of an evil scrunched up face and fingers curled near your cheeks.]

So, the witch kind of liked Evil Betty and so they were friends now.
So, the witch cursed Evil Betty back to being nice again and all the other cursed people back to being nice again.

The witch's heart got bigger and bigger and they lived happily ever after.

The end.

-- Macie Miazga 1/18/2012
