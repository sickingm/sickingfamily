To Elaine

There once was a beer-drinker named Elaine
Who was quite fond of hops and barley grain.
    After every Bud Light
    Try as she might
From having another she could not refrain.

There once was a girl named Elaine
Who for skinny guys had great disdain.
    She liked her men large
    As big as a barge
Too bad she was too young for John Wayne.

When Jinny started with her first labor pain
Amid the December snow and freezing rain
    She said, "This baby'll be funny
    With disposition quite sunny.
So I think we should name her Elaine."

There once was a girl named Elaine
Who made comments both funny & insane.
    She said, "To tell you the truth
    I might be uncouth,
But I gotta say whatever's in my brain."

We have a cute sister named Elaine
Who is anything but a Plain Jane.
    Even though she's 50
    She sure looks nifty
And still will when she needs a walking cane.

I'm sorry I can't be with you today
But there is something I just have to say:
    I love you a lot,
    More than even a tater tot
And there couldn't be a better sister, no way!

Happy Birthday, Elaine!

Love, Paul

-- Paul 12/19/07
